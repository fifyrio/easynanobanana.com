## A Simple Method to Get the Best Results

Anyone can use the tool effectively. Here's the approach that gives the most realistic results.

### 1. Start with a clean, well-lit photo

Tie your hair back if possible. Keep your face visible, with no heavy shadows.

![Start with a clean, well-lit photo](/images/blogs/Start-with-a-clean-well-lit%20photo.png)

### 2. Choose your dream hairstyle

This can be from Pinterest, TikTok, Instagram, or a photo of a celebrity.
Straight, wavy, curly, bangs, wolf cut, balayage—anything.

![Choose your dream hairstyle](/images/blogs/choose-your-dream-hairstyle.png)

### 3. Generate the AI hairstyle preview

Upload your photo → upload your reference → the AI maps the hairstyle to your face in seconds.

You'll get a realistic preview showing how the hairstyle looks on your actual face shape, with proper lighting and natural blending.

![Generate the AI hairstyle preview](/images/blogs/Generate-the-AI-hairstyle-preview.png)

### 4. Compare and evaluate

Ask yourself:

* Does the length match my jawline?
* Do the bangs overwhelm my forehead?
* Does the overall style look balanced?
* Does the colour suit my skin tone?

Side-by-side comparisons make decisions easy.

### 5. Bring the AI preview to your stylist

This reduces miscommunication by **showing your actual face** with the desired style.
Most stylists love it because it gives them a clear direction.
